Why is there a "How to Use" page?

With the powerful tools that we have created, we tried to make them as easy to use as possible. With anything like this, we decided to give you, the user, some best practices and features to maximize your experience on our site.

Below are three helpful tips on Filtering, Resetting, and Hovering. Enjoy!




Filtering

In the image to the left, you can see the filtering options that are available on both of our dashboards. When filtering down by team or positon, you must keep at least one of the players selected or no graphes or points will be seen in the dashboard. One may think by selecting the team, all of the players in that team will appear, but that is not the case.

To avoid scrolling to find our players or teams, you can simply type who or what you are looking for in the search area in the upper right hand corner of each filter as seen on the right.




Resetting

If you hit some filters either selecting or deselecting, you can use the back arrow, as shown in the image to the left, to get back to where you where. The forward arrow does the opposite.

After filtering through all of your selection for a position or player, you might want to start a new search. The third item which is an arrow with a perpendicular line will reset everything in the dashboard.

If you would like to save something you have created, the rectangle with the arrow pointing down allows you to save an image or pdf of your results.




Hovering

These dashboards are meant to be interactive! When looking at the points on the graph, you can hover over the point to see more detailed information such as those seen in the image on the left. You can also select the player's name on the left and it will highlight the point on the graph where that player is located.

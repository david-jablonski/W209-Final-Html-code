Football Fantasy League Helper

Our website leverages the vast amount of real-time information generated by experts into a format that you, the user, can easily synthesize and ultimately equip you with insights to execute an educated decision for your fantasy football team.

Terminology

Expert Consensus Ranking (ECR): Each week, a team of experts will rank their top NFL Football players when considering expected points and each player's ranking will be averaged from all the inputs.

Projected Points: An average of the point totals for each player which is an educated guess about the next week's performance of that player.

Value Over Replacement (VOR): Demonstrates a player's contribution to their team, as compared to an imaginary "replacement player".

Points Rank: A discrete ranking based on the players Projected Points with respect to the player's position.
